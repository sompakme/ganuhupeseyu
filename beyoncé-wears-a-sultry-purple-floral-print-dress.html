Beyonc Wears a Sultry Purple Floral-Print Dress

 Posted on September 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  335 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Beyoncé has come back to Instagram after three months of silence.

The music superstar shared a new outfit post yesterday, making her first appearance on the social platform since her appearance at the 94th Academy Awards in March. In the sultry pics, the star poses in front of a red background while wearing a curve-hugging floral-print dress by Paco Rabanne, featuring a thigh-high slit and ruffled detailing on the sides, and showing a hint of a black lacy bra.

She accessorized the midi-length piece with black heeled sandals by Alexander McQueen, matching floral-print gloves, diamond hoop earrings, a diamond bracelet and ring, a black wristlet bag.

The "Be Alive" singer has been radio silent since the Oscars ceremony, when she performed her song from the King Richard soundtrack, which was also nominated this year. While performing at a tennis court in Compton, CA, the star wore a sequined sheer dress with feather trim by David Koma. She and her backup dancers were all dressed in head-to-toe neon yellow for the breathtaking moment.

The "Formation" singer also wore two stunning full-length gowns on Oscar night. To attend the ceremony itself, Queen Bey chose a daffodil-yellow gown with a sweetheart neckline, which included a sweeping off-the-shoulder taffeta cape and matching gloves. She accessorized with 150-carat waterfall earrings and a 35-carat diamond pinky ring by Lorraine Schwartz, bright yellow platform heels by Delcore, and a tennis ball-shaped purse, in honor of the biopic about tennis stars Venus and Serena Williams.

She later changed into a sparklier look for her Gold Party, wearing an almost completely sheer gown with strategically-placed crystal embellishments and a high slit. She wore the naked-look gown with a matching veil, clear strappy heels, narrow black sunglasses, and a diamond Tiffany and Co. necklace.
