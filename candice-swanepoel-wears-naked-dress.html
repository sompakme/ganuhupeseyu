Candice Swanepoel wears naked dress

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  152 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Another day, another celebrity wearing a naked dress on the red carpet. At last night's Tony Awards, Candice Swanepoel adopted the seemingly ubiquitous trend in a Prabal Gurung gown.

The Victoria's Secret Angel shimmered in the Bordeaux strapless dress, which was adorned with hand-embroidered floating crystals; the bodice was also decorated in intricate beading. The lower part of the dress was completely sheer to reveal the model's full briefs.

Swanepoel brought the man behind her gown, Prabal Gurung, as her date. On Instagram, the designer referred to the model as his "muse".
