Pippa Movie (2023): Pippa is a war drama film based on the book The Burning Chaffees by Brigadier Balram Singh Mehta, who was a tank commander in the 1971 Indo-Pak war. The film is directed by Raja Krishna Menon and stars Ishaan Khatter, Mrunal Thakur, Priyanshu Painyuli, and Soni Razdan in the lead roles. The film is produced by Ronnie Screwvala, Siddharth Roy Kapur, and Raja Krishna Menon.

The film is set in the backdrop of the battle of Garibpur, where a small group of Indian soldiers held off a large Pakistani force for 48 hours with the help of their tanks, which they named Pippa. The film is a tribute to the bravery and sacrifice of the Indian Army and the bond of brotherhood among the soldiers.

The film was originally scheduled to be released in theatres on December 2, 2022, but due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the makers decided to opt for a digital release on Amazon Prime Video. The film premiered on November 10, 2023, coinciding with the birthday of Ishaan Khatter, who plays the role of Captain Balram Singh Mehta.
