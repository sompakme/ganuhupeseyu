5 best Pink Nike Dunk sneakers planned for 2023

 Posted on August 13, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1050 words  |  Martina Birk

In the world of sneaker fashion, the Nike Dunk has established itself as a timeless product. Now, the Dunk takes on a bold and vibrant transformation with its pink colorway, offering a fresh and eye-catching option for sneaker enthusiasts.

Combining the classic silhouette of the Dunk with a feminine touch, the below-mentioned pink Dunks showcase a perfect blend of style and self-expression. Whether one is a fashion-forward trendsetter or looking to add a pop of color to their wardrobe, these sneakers offer a statement-making option that embraces individuality and embraces the power of pink. As such, here's a list of 5 best “Pink” Nike Dunk sneakers of 2023.

For 2023, the pink hue is mainly seen in Nike Dunk Low sneakers

1) Nike Dunk Low “Pink Paisley”

The Dunk Low “Pink Paisley” is a new colorway of the classic Dunk Low silhouette. The paisley print is the design inspiration of the Dunk Low "Pink Paisley" design inspiration, which has been a major theme for the Swoosh in 2023.

The shoe features a white leather upper with pink paisley accents on the Swoosh and heel tab. The colorway is called White/Medium Soft Pink/Pearl Pink, whose rubber outsole and white midsole balance the style of the shoe. The sneaker is designed exclusively in women's sizes and was released on February 7, 2023, at select retailers and Nike.com. The retail price for the shoe is $110 USD.

2) Nike Dunk Low "52" Grey and Pink

The Dunk Low "52" Grey and Pink is one of the most recent colors that is set to appear in 2023. The sneakers is part of the 52nd anniversary of the footwear label, which features release of other sneaker models like the Zoom Vomero 5 "52."

Although Nike has not yet made an official announcement about it, the Dunk Low "52" Grey and Pink is anticipated to release at some point in 2023 as a part of the company's 52nd anniversary celebration. The pair's cost is also yet unclear, but it is anticipated to be comparable to previous Dunk Lows, which typically sell for roughly $110 USD.

3) Nike Dunk Low "Pink Corduroy"

The Dunk Low "Pink Corduroy" is a new iteration of the Nike Dunk Low silhouette that is set to release in the coming months. The design of the shoe features a clean white leather base with a white perforated toe box, midfoot panel, and heel, matching its stitched midsole, plain tongue, and synthetic lining.

The shoe overlays pastel pink accents all over the sneakers, landing across the laces, tongue tab, rubber outsole, and sock-liner. The release date for the shoe is tentatively scheduled for Fall 2023. The shoe is priced at $110 USD.

4) Nike Dunk Low “Pink Foam”

The Dunk Low “Pink Foam” features a shaggy suede upper in a Pink/Grey makeover. The sneaker has a white perforated toe box, midfoot panel, and heel, matching its crisp stitched midsole, plain tongue, and synthetic lining. Addtionally, the shoe has pastel pink hues throughout the laces, tongue tab, rubber outsole, and sock-liner.

The shoe was released on April 6, 2023, and is a women's exclusive sneaker. The retail price for each pair is $120 USD. The shoes can be purchased from Nike’s SNKRS app and a few of its affiliated retail merchants.

5) Nike Dunk Low "Strawberry and Peach"

The Dunk Low "Strawberry and Peach" sneakers feature a full leather construction with a soft orange shade across the sides and toes. While strawberry elements are applied to the overlay the tongue labels, systems, and sockliner, the shoe chooses a light orange tint for its sides and toes. The lacing system, rubber midsoles, and Swoosh emblems all include cream coloring. Lastly, a pink outsole completes the new design.

The shoes for grade schoolers will release soon in 2023. The shoes are priced at $90 USD and have a style code of DH9765-200.

Pink Nike Dunk sneakers effortlessly capture attention and empower individuals to express their unique style. With its vibrant hue and iconic Dunk silhouette, these sneakers are a powerful fashion statement that transcends gender norms and embraces the beauty of individuality.
