Career Beginnings and Breakthrough

Drew Carey’s journey to wealth began in the late 1980s when he started his career as a stand-up comedian. After a successful appearance on “The Tonight Show Starring Johnny Carson,” Carey’s career took off, leading to a starring role in his own sitcom, “The Drew Carey Show,” which aired from 1995 to 2004. This show was a significant contributor to his early financial success.

Transition to Game Show Hosting

In 2007, Carey took over as the host of the iconic game show “The Price Is Right,” following in the footsteps of the legendary Bob Barker. This role not only increased his visibility but also significantly boosted his earnings, contributing to a substantial portion of his net worth.
