Biography

Phillip Forest Lewitski (born June 9, 1997) is a renowned Canadian actor and TV personality. He gained popularity for his exceptional portrayal of Apollo 4 in the acclaimed TV show Utopia Falls. 

He received critical acclaim for his remarkable performance as Lincoln in Wildhood, resulting in a well-deserved nomination for Best Actor at the Canadian Screen Awards. 

Furthermore, Phillip Lewitski has showcased his talent through recurring roles in hit series like Vikings and Supernatural, and fans can eagerly anticipate his appearance in the highly anticipated Apple TV+ series Masters of the Air.
