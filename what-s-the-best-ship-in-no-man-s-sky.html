How do you get the S tier ship in no man's sky?

Use An Economy Scanner And Find A Trading Post

After landing in the Trading post, you will have to wait for the game to start spawning different ships, eventually, you'll see No man's Sky S-Class ships. You can scan them in the air to find out. If none appear, just reload the game in that same spot!

How do you get a better starship in no man's sky?

Buy Starships With Units

Although there are more challenging ways to find Starships if you're low on Units, the easiest way to get a new Starship in No Man's Sky is to buy it. You can purchase Starships from anywhere they land in the Universe, including Outposts, Space Stations, and Trading Posts.
