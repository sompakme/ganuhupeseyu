Kodak Black Celebrates His Birthday With New Bling

 Posted on July 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  365 words  |  Martina Birk

Kodak Black recently celebrated his 26th birthday at the club. He also got some new jewelry from Miami jeweler Diamond Talk, showing off his new bracelet in a recent clip. The rapper has a lot to celebrate recently. He's hot off of the release of his latest album, Pistolz & Pearlz and just recently opened a new boutique in Miami.

Kodak Black has also taken part in various collaborations as of late. Earlier this month, Kodak Black's track alongside French Montana, titled "I Can't Lie," was released. He also recently appeared on the soundtrack of the film Fast X. He teamed up with NLE Choppa, BTS' Jimin, JVKE, and Muni Long for “Angel Pt. 1." His recently-released LP also features Vvsnce, Est Gee, GorditoFlo, and more.

The rapper opened his Miami boutique last month, and held a grand opening event alongside Supa Cindy. He took part in a ribbon-cutting ceremony, and fans were treated to swag from the event and a DJ set from Showtime. In April, Kodak Black also revealed his desires to become more well-known as a philanthropist. “People care to be a gangsta,” he explained, “They care for n***as looking at them like they some type of gangsta." I only worry about being a good son and making sure my mom is proud of me. Making sure my family proud of me.” 

He also stood up for Donald Trump in April, claiming that he thinks his haters are "on some other s**t." Kodak Black explained his thoughts, saying "They are trying to get Trump out the way cause Trump a stand-up n***a." "[Joe] Biden will let a n***a do anything. Trump is a real n***a. He’s a soldier," he said, "It ain’t how he snapped for a n***a. It ain’t even about that. Trump already had big baggage. Luggage. Y’all get off Trump, man.”
