Why does Piney wear a denim cut?

Kurt Sutter is quoted as saying that Piney wears a denim “cut” because he's “old school.” The first nine couldn't afford leather when they started out. Because Piney has always been a mistrustful curmudgeon, he never made the jump to leather.
