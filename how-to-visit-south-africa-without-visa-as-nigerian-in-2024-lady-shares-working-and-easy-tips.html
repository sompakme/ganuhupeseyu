Some Nigerians dream of visiting South Africa but worry about the visa process.

In a video shared by @sassyfunke, a young lady has shared her tips and tricks on how to make it to South Africa without worrying about visa.

She said that the key to effective success in this regard was taking advantage of the 90-day visa-free option that South Africa granted to Ghana from the 1st of November, 2023.

Apply and move abroad easily through the Canada agro-food pilot program

Meanwhile, Legit.ng earlier reported that applications for the Canada Agro-Food Pilot Program are set to recommence on January 1, 2024. The Canada Agro-Food Pilot Program allows skilled people to apply and work in Canada's agricultural sector.

Through this pathway, people skilled in the agricultural sector can apply for and get permanent residency permits to work as farm labourers, livestock labourers, butchers, harvesting labourers, labourers in food and beverage processing and more.

The program, which was introduced in 2020 by the Canadian government, has been extended to May 2024.
