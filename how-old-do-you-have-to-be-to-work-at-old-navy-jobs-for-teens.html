How Old do you have to be to Work at Old Navy? (Jobs For Teens)

 Posted on October 2, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1208 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Starting your career is an exciting journey, and there are so many amazing opportunities out there. You are entering a new chapter in your life with so many different professions to choose from, so let’s make sure that you make the right decisions.

So, you’re interested in working for Old Navy, but you’re not sure that you’re old enough to? No worries, I’m going to take an in-depth look at the question… How old do you have to be to work at Old Navy? 

In basic terms, the minimum age to work at Old Navy is 16; however, this does not apply to every department, so let’s find out more…

Types of Employment

Store

If you are under 18, you can get a job at Old Navy as a Brand Associate, a Stock Associate, or as Loss Prevention Personnel. For some of these positions, you won’t need a diploma or any other educational background or experience. However, for others, you will need qualifications, knowledge, and experience.

Read more: How Much Does Old Navy Pay? (Job Positions, Benefits And More…)

Brand Associate

This involves helping customers, giving advice, and sharing your knowledge of the product, as well as giving good customer service. You will be helping customers find the merchandise they are looking for. You will also give fashion advice to customers and get them interested in the brand and the store. 

To excel in this role, you will need: 

  • Excellent communication skills
  • Be good at problem solving
  • Quick learner
  • Friendly and helpful personality 

To work as a Brand Associate, you need to be a minimum of 16 years old, and you don’t need any special education or experience for this position.

Stock Associate

This is similar to working as a brand associate, the difference between the two is the work schedule, and a Stock Associate mainly handles the stock part of things, such as keeping the store inventory neat and tidy, and making sure that what is needed is there. 

Great skills to have for this position are:

  • Good attention to detail
  • Friendly and helpful personality
  • Being able to easily do physical tasks
  • Great organizational skills

You will need to be a minimum of 16 years old, and you don’t need any special education or experience for this role.

Loss Prevention

In this role, your main objective is to prevent theft and damage. You watch security cameras, the customers, and even the employees to see if there are any signs or suspicions of theft. 

Great skills to have for this position are:

  • Good observation skills
  • Patience
  • Good at Communicating
  • Being able to think clearly in stressful situations

To work in loss and prevention, you need to be a minimum of 16 years old; however, you need to have a high school diploma or GED education. Experience isn’t necessarily required for this position, but it is preferred. 

Other in store positions at Old Navy

District Manager – responsible for the stores in the region that they are assigned to. 

General Manager – responsible for the store or department they are employed in. 

Supervisor – “the team leader” of a group of staff. 

Merchandising 

To work in Merchandising at Old Navy, you will need a background in Business and Financing. 

Merchandising positions at Old Navy

Visual Merchandiser – responsible for decorating the store and displaying the merchandise. 

Buyer – responsible for purchasing the merchandise 

Merchandiser – responsible for creating an eyecatching display of the product. They are also responsible for inventory and finance.

Production 

In production, you make and create the products. 

Production positions at Old Navy

Production Development – This department is responsible for making/ recreating products.

Associate Production Manager- An Associate production manager is responsible for running the production of his/her production line. 

Marketing

To work in marketing at Old Navy, you need an educational background or experience in Marketing. 

Marketing positions at Old Navy

Associate Marketing Manager – responsible for strengthening the brand’s name, e.g., campaigning.

Art Director – responsible for the artistic aspects of the store and the brand in the media. 

Graphic Design – creates the marketing designs.

How old do you have to be to work at Old Navy? – Salary 

The amount of money you make an hour depends on your experience, age, education, and the state you work in. The federal minimum wage for people under 20 is $4.25 per hour. 

Working as a Brand Associate at Old Navy at age 16 without any work experience or higher education, you are looking at making somewhere between $8 and $16 per hour.

Working as a Stock Associate, you’ll make around $11 – $14 per hour. If you’re employed as loss prevention, your salary will be around $20 per hour. 

You’ve found a job you would like to apply for? That’s great. Let’s now take a look at…

How to apply for a job at Old Navy

Fortunately, the process is not complicated at all. You apply through their website Old Navy Careers | Gap Inc., or you can apply in person. You submit your application, and that’s it! Then all you have to do is wait for their response which should come within a week via email or telephone.

How to prepare for a job interview at Old Navy  

Outfit

Since you’re interviewing for a position at a clothing store, it’s best to dress smart/casual with well-matched clothes, such as dark slacks paired with a button-down shirt and a blazer to match. For the shoes, wear clean, comfortable closed shoes.    

 It’s important to avoid oversized, dirty, and ill-fitting clothes. 

Common Old Navy Interview questions:

  • How did you hear about this position?
  • Why do you want to work at Old Navy?
  • What do you know about Old Navy?
  • What are your career goals for the future?

It helps to go over possible questions that might come up in the interview, so when they ask you a question, you already know what to say, which will make you look more confident and help calm your nerves.

How old do you have to be to work at Old Navy – Final Thoughts

If you’re under 18 and want to get your career started, or maybe you just want to earn some cash to buy something you’ve always wanted. Then Old Navy is a great option because even at the age of 16, they hire Brand Associates for an average of $10 per hour, Stock Associates for an average of $12 per hour, and Loss Prevention for around $20 per hour.
