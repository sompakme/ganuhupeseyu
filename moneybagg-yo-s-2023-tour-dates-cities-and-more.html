Moneybagg Yo's 2023 Tour: Dates, Cities, And More

 Posted on August 16, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  803 words  |  Larita Shotwell

In a momentous announcement for hip-hop fans, Moneybagg Yo has released the dates and locations for his highly anticipated 2023 tour. The Memphis rapper will be blazing a trail through major cities across the country, starting in Orlando, Florida, and ending in his hometown of Memphis, Tennessee.

Moneybagg Yo Tour 2023: Kicking Off In Florida

ATLANTA, GEORGIA - JULY 16: Rapper Moneybagg Yo performs onstage during 2022 Hot 107.9 Birthday Bash at Center Parc Credit Union Stadium at Georgia State University on July 16, 2022 in Atlanta, Georgia. (Photo by Paras Griffin/Getty Images)

The Larger Than Life tour kicks off on August 3rd in Orlando, Florida, at the Amway Center, swiftly followed by a performance in Atlanta, Georgia, at the State Farm Arena on August 4th. Moneybagg Yo will then head north, delighting fans in Charlotte, North Carolina, with a show at the Spectrum Center on August 6th.

Moneybagg Yo Tour 2023: The East Coast Swing

NEW YORK, NEW YORK - SEPTEMBER 23: Music artist Moneybagg Yo performs during 2022 Rolling Loud New York at Citi Field on September 23, 2022 in New York City. (Photo by Arturo Holmes/Getty Images)

From there, Moneybagg Yo will take on the East Coast, with a special performance at Terminal 5 in New York City on August 8th. Philadelphia and Washington D.C. are the next stops, with shows at the Liacouras Center and the Capital One Arena on August 10th and 11th respectively.

Heading To The Midwest

ATLANTA, GA - NOVEMBER 29: (EXCLUSIVE COVERAGE) Rapper Moneybagg Yo performs onstage during Lil Baby & Friends concert to promote the new release of Lil Baby's new album "Street Gossip" at Coca-Cola Roxy on November 29, 2018 in Atlanta, Georgia. (Photo by Paras Griffin/Getty Images)

By mid-August, the tour will move to the Midwest. Music lovers in Nashville, Tennessee, can catch the act at the Bridgestone Arena on August 13th. The rapper will then make his way to Indianapolis, Cincinnati, and Chicago for performances on August 17th, 18th, and 19th respectively. On August 25th, he'll head to St. Louis to grace fans with his presence at Chaifetz Arena.

West Coast Tour Dates

INGLEWOOD, CALIFORNIA - MARCH 05: Rapper Moneybagg Yoperforms onstage during day 3 of Rolling Loud Los Angeles at Hollywood Park Grounds on March 05, 2023 in Inglewood, California. (Photo by Scott Dudelson/Getty Images)

The tour continues west, with performances scheduled for Denver, Los Angeles, Las Vegas, Seattle, and Oakland through the end of August and early September.

A Star-Studded Support Line Up

Notably, the support lineup will vary, featuring artists such as Finesse2tymes, Sexxy Red, Luh Tyler, Big Boogie, and YTB FATT. These artists will add a dynamic and diverse range of styles to the tour's performances.

Moneybagg Yo Tour 2023: Wrapping Up In The South

MEMPHIS, TENNESSEE - MAY 01: Moneybagg Yo goes into the crowd during the Beale Street Music Festival at Liberty Park on May 01, 2022 in Memphis, Tennessee. (Photo by Astrida Valigorsky/Getty Images)

The tour will wrap up in the South, with performances in Birmingham, Fort Worth, Houston, and Richmond. The grand finale will be a performance at the FedEx Forum in Moneybagg Yo's hometown of Memphis, Tennessee, on September 30th.
