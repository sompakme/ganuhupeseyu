Rosamund Pike would like to see a Gone Girl sequel

 Posted on August 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  336 words  |  Aldo Pusey

    Rosamund Pike has revealed that she'd like to reprise her role of Amy Elliot-Dunne in a sequel. The actress, who has received critical acclaim and awards nominations for the role, told reporters that she would like to see what happened to the family ten years later. "Maybe, ten years down the road," she said at the Bafta Los Angeles Tea Party on Sunday. "I'd think I'd want… to see what happens when the child is about ten." is an adaptation of Gillian Flynn's best-selling novel of the same name.
