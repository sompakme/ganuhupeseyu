Uvalde, Texas – Irma Garcia’s husband, Joe Garcia killed in Tuesday’s mass shooting at Robb Elementary School had a heart attack and died at the age of 48 years on 26 May 2022. News Unzip team has been in touch with the family now grieving two losses.

How Irma Garcia’s Husband Joe Garcia Died?

On 26 May 2022, Guadalupe “Joe” Garcia reportedly suffered a heart attack after dropping off flowers at his wife’s memorial. His spouse, Irma Garcia was one of the two teachers killed in Tuesday’s mass shooting at Robb Elementary School. The high school sweethearts leave behind four children.

Wednesday night John Martinez, Joe Garcia, and Irma Garcia’s nephew told me the family has described this week as a nightmare. Martinez says Joe Garcia was a family man who would do anything and everything for his four kids (2 sons and 2 daughters).

Meet Joe Garcia & Irma Garcia Children (Cristian Garcia, Jose Garcia, Lyliana Garcia & Alysandra Garcia)

Joe Garcia and Irma Garcia’s eldest son Cristian Garcia is completing Marine boot camp. Irma Garcia’s second son Jose Garcia is pursuing his graduation from the Texas State Univeristy. Her elder daughter Lyliana Garcia will attend her high school this year. Joe Garcia’s youngest daughter Alysandra Garcia is studying in 7th grade in 2022.

Irma Garcia was an amazing wife, mother, and fourth-grade teacher. He says her body was found holding another student protecting that child who was also killed in the shooting. Martinez says he was not in a place to give us an interview today and understandably so but he wants the world to know his Thea died a hero. Her name is Irma Garcia.
