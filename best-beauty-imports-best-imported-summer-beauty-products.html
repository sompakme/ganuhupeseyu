Blended with flowers, woods and exotic herbs from the Yucatan Peninsula, Coqui Coqui Perfumes are mixed in the colonial town of Valladolid, and have a mystical quality about them. The tobacco cologne is made for “shamans, medicine men or cowboys” but smells sexy on women, too.
