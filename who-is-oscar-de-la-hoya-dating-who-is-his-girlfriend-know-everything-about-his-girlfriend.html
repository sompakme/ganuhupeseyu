From the Ring to the Promoter’s Chair

Post his retirement in 2009, De La Hoya ventured into boxing promotion and founded Golden Boy Promotions. And he didn’t stop there – Golden Boy Promotions even dipped its toes into MMA, adding another feather to De La Hoya’s cap.

In a surprising turn of events, De La Hoya was granted Mexican citizenship in 2002, emphasizing his dual citizenship with the United States and Mexico. This dual identity became a symbol of his pride and cultural ties to both nations.
