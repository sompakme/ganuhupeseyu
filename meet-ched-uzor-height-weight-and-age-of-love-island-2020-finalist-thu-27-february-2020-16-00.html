Airing from Sunday, January 12th 2020 on ITV2, Love Island arrived to hot up the British Winter! The brand new instalment of Love Island ended with a finale on Sunday, February 23rd.

Keeping the temperatures soaring on Love Island, the series 6 villa was located in scorching South Africa. And a brand new batch of Islanders made themselves very much at home.

Siannise, Callum, Shaughna, Luke T and co settled into the series pretty quickly, but the series wasn’t without its surprises including the controversial Casa Amor episodes.

Ched Uzor was a newbie during Casa Amor on Love Island 2020. So, let’s get to know Ched: Height to Instagram!

Meet Ched

One of 12 newcomers to Casa Amor on Love Island 2020, Ched is 23 years old and hails from Suffolk. The scaffolder entered the villa in a huge twist during episode 20!

By the looks of things, Ched wasn’t exactly shy going onto the show. According to ITV, he said he “rates his looks eight out of 10 and says his arms are his best feature.”

Perhaps it was his confidence that swept Jess Gale off of her feet and got the pair to the 2020 final. Ched and Jess came in fourth place on the show.

Find Ched on Instagram under the handle @ched.uzor where he has over 224,000 followers as of February 2020.

Ched Uzor – height and weight

Although Ched’s height isn’t confirmed, compared to the other Casa Amor lads, we can assume that he’s probably around 6 ft 3 to 6 ft 4.

His fellow Casa Amor Islander, Josh Kempton, is 6 ft 1 and it looks as though Ched’s even taller than him by a couple of inches

Speaking to ITV, Ched said that he’s willing to do whatever it takes to find his ideal girl:

It’s not Friend Island, if I fancy someone and she likes me and not you, it’s not my fault!

‘Tall, dark and handsome’ is what pretty much every girl in the villa said they were looking for, so it was safe to say that Ched was a welcome addition to the villa in episode 20.

Likewise, Ched’s weight is not readily available on the internet. Then again, judging compared to the official weight of other Islanders, Ched could clock in at around 100-115kg.

There are some crazy gym routines on his Instagram where he squats 210kg with a pause rep and busts out 15 reps on the dumbbell shoulder press with 50kg weights.
