Kelly Osbourne's mysterious leg brace

 Posted on August 16, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  153 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Kelly Osbourne drew concern from worried fans on Saturday evening as she stepped out wearing a leg brace in Los Angeles. Later on Instagram, the TV presenter took a shot of her "Robocop boot" and revealed she had suffered a stress fracture.

"#StressFractures are the worst! At least I get a cool #RoboCopBoot", Kelly captioned the snap.

The 29-year-old star was attending the 2014 Trevor Project NextGen Prom with pal Amanda Golombek and had styled her recently shorn off hair in to a stylish quiff.

Wearing a gorgeous black lace dress, Kelly showed off her svelte figure -  which she maintains through healthy eating and regular workouts with a personal trainer. After Kelly split from her fiancé Matthew Mosshart she was said to have gained weight. But she disputed this - and shared her annoyance over the media's obsession with her figure.
