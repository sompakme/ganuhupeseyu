He is also well-known for his signature mask.

Why does Orville Peck wear a mask?

Orville Peck, real name, Daniel Pitout, was born on January 6, 1988, in Johannesburg, South Africa.

Orville is a Canadian-based country musician who is famously known for wearing a fringed mask and has never shown his face publicly.

Explaining the reason why he wears a mask, he was quoted as saying: "The only reason I don’t talk about it in depth is not because I want to dodge any questions, but because I want people to have their own take on it.

"I don’t want to lay it out and pin it down. I just don’t think that’s important."
