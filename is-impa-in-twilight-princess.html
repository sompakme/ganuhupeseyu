Is Impa in Twilight Princess?

 Posted on August 15, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  997 words  |  Aldo Pusey
During The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess, Impa herself is not seen but a character named Impaz who is also a Sheikah mention that she was named after the founder of the Hidden Village and since this village is said to be the old Kakarico Village found in Ocarina of Time, it means that she is named after Impa.

What happened to Impa?

When Hyrule was under attack by Ganon's forces in The Legend of Zelda, Princess Zelda ordered her old nursemaid Impa to secretly flee and find the one who could save them. Ganon soon found out and sent his men after Impa. Though she managed to stay out of their reach for a long time, she was eventually captured.

Who is Impaz waiting for?

Impaz is a 70-year-old woman, and the only resident left in the run-down Hidden Village within Twilight Princess. She cannot leave the village due to royal decree and is the last remaining Sheikah resident; Impaz is said to have to wait for the "messenger to the heavens" so she can give them the Ancient Sky Book.

Where is Hidden Village Twilight Princess?

The Hidden Village is a location featured in Twilight Princess. It is one of the last locations revealed within the game. The village itself is found within Eldin Province and is situated to the northern trail of the Bridge of Eldin.

What do I do with the owl statues in Twilight Princess?

The owl statue is located just northeast of the bridge; control it and bring it down. If you look up, you'll notice some vines hanging down. You need to position the owl statue so it is right under the vines. Use the clawshot to latch onto the vines and then fall down onto the statue.

How do you get the medicine scent in Twilight Princess?

The Medicine Scent is a scent from The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess. It can be found in the Medical Clinic after moving a crate.
