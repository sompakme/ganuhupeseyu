Kylie Jenner Keeps Wearing These Levis Jeans That Are on Sale

 Posted on July 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  454 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Many of us spend years looking for the perfect pair of jeans. I can’t even begin to count the number of times I’ve squeezed myself into too-tight, too-stiff denim at the store, convinced the jeans will change my life (dramatic, I know), only for them to collect dust in my closet; the pants meant to be an everyday staple were actually wildly uncomfortable. But it seems that Kylie Jenner has found the pair, and they’re so affordable and shopper-loved that I might just grab one for myself. Who knows, maybe this could be my perfect pair, too?

The star kicked off the summer in Levi’s Low Pro jeans, and immediately I took note. The slightly-baggy jeans looked amazing with everything she wore, from a simple white tee and ballet flats to a darling, cottagecore-inspired blouse. And this week, she brought the style into fall when she rocked them once again, this time with a classic V-neck sweater. 

And while a good pair of jeans I’ll wear over and over again is something I’m willing to splurge on, I was pleasantly surprised to learn that I don’t have to with these. Because following Cyber Monday, Jenner’s go-to Low Pro style is on sale at Amazon for up to 60 percent off.

While the name might imply that the jeans sit low on the hips, these are actually more of a mid-rise style, which one shopper found to be the perfect medium. They wrote that they sit high enough that “no crack shows when bending down,” without being so high in the front you can’t eat or breathe in them. “[The] material is not too thick but sturdy [and my] ass looks great,” they added. Another person agreed, calling the mid-rise a “game changer,” noting that it doesn’t suffocate their belly but does “suck [them] in perfectly.”

But beyond the rise, the Low Pro style is popular for its roomy but not totally oversized fit. “I feel hugged and supported, instead of squished and constricted,” said one shopper who added that they “slid on” and “fit perfectly.” “These jeans are a dream. They are incredibly comfortable, making them great for accomplishing all the things while still looking stylish,” raved another fan. Plus, “they have a magical way of making your rear end look fantastic,” they added. One other customer loved the “quality,” 100-percent cotton fabric and “classic style” which gets them “lots of compliments.” They added: “I’m thinking of getting another pair when these are in the laundry!”

The comfy Levi’s Jenner wears on repeat (and that more than 1,400 Amazon customer love) start at just $32 this Cyber Week, so make sure to add a pair (or two) to your cart.
